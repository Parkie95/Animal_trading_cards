Dog

The Beatles song 'A Day in the Life' has a frequency only dogs can hear.

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Lifespan: 10 - 13 years
  • Height: 15 – 110 cm (At Shoulder)
  • Gestation period: 58 – 68 days

Dogs are fun, friendly and playful animals that have been domesticated by mankind for many years. They are very loyal and loving. Dogs are thought to have been domesticated somewhere in Europe or western Siberia, between 18,800 - 32,100 years ago.
